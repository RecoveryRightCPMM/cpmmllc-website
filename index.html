Core Capabilities

Where specialized billing meets aggressive recovery.

We do not offer broad revenue cycle management (RCM) or simple data-entry billing. CPMM is a highly specialized recovery engine built exclusively for high-dollar, complex claims.

🚗

Motor Vehicle Accident (MVA) Claims

Auto insurance claims are notoriously labor-intensive, often involving complex coordination between medical providers, auto carriers, PIP/MedPay limits, and third-party attorneys.

  • PIP & MedPay Coordination: Tracking down accurate policy limits, proper claim numbers, and identifying primary vs. secondary auto coverages.
  • Third-Party Liability (TPL): Securing proper verification of auto insurance details and managing subrogation liens cleanly.
  • Attorney Liaison: Standardizing communication with legal representatives to protect provider medical liens before settlement distributions.
Scope: Carrier-Level Appeals Only · Commercial & Auto Coverages
🛠️

Workers' Compensation Recovery

Workplace injury claims feature strict, unforgiving state-level administrative guidelines, employer authorization bottlenecks, and specialized medical fee schedules.

  • Employer Verification: Bypassing delays by hunting down exact injury reports, carrier reference IDs, and authorized adjusters.
  • Fee Schedule Adjudication: Auditing payments to counter underpayments and ensuring carriers reimburse fully under statutory regulations.
  • Contested Claim Navigation: Building structured case histories to defend medical necessity and secure payouts for authorized treatments.
Scope: Carrier-Level Appeals Only · Commercial Coverages
⚖️

Aggressive Internal Appeals

When high-dollar commercial claims face technical, wrongful, or silent denials, we analyze the carrier rules and launch highly technical internal appeals.

  • Denial Pattern Auditing: Rooting out silent systemic downcoding or automated batch denials driven by algorithmic processing.
  • Level 1 & Level 2 Appeals: Authoring comprehensive, evidence-based appeal packets loaded with medical documentation and statutory support.
  • Timely Filing Defenses: Reconstructive data assembly to challenge claims wrongfully denied under tight administrative deadlines.
Scope: Limited to Insurer Levels 1 & 2 · No Arbitration/Litigation
🔍

Denial Analysis & Single Case Agreements

Proactive positioning for complex out-of-network scenarios, unexpected medical necessity gridlocks, and localized coordination-of-benefits disputes.

  • Single Case Agreements (SCA): Assisting with the administrative tracking and validation of specialized out-of-network provider arrangements.
  • Coordination of Benefits (COB): Unraveling conflicting coverage data to pin down the correct commercial payer.
  • Technical Auditing: Reviewing historic claim histories to isolate recurring coding loops causing administrative rejections.
Scope: Commercial Payers Only · Zero Government Payer Support
